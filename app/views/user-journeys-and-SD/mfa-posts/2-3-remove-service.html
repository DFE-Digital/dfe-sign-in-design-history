MFA - Remove a service journey

User journey for when an apporver removes a service from their account

From: DfE Sign-in Team

Published: 3rd October 2023

Last updated: 4th October 2023

Journey overview

Approvers in DfE Sign-in have the ability to remove services from their own account. Therefore it is necessary to warn approvers of the implications removing a service might have.

Because MFA is implemented at a service level, removing a service can result in a user not being required to use MFA to access their account. Therefore this journey shows the journey of removing a service and the warning which informs users of the implications of removing a service which requires MFA.

Who can access this journey?

  • All approver users in DfE Sign-in
