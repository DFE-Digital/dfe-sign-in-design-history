MFA - Re-setup MFA journey

User journey for when a user has had their MFA rest and is required to set it up again

From: DfE Sign-in Team

Published: 3rd October 2023

Last updated: 4th October 2023

Journey overview

This journey shows the process of a user setting up their MFA again after having their MFA reset.

Users who have had their MFA reset due to the fact they cannot use or access their MFA method will be required to setup MFA again after this has been approved when they next login to their account.

Who can access this journey?

  • DfE Sign-in users who have required to have their MFA reset
