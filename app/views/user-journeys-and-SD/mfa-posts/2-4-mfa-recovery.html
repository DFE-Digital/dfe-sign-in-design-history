MFA - Recovery journey

User journey for when a user cannot access their chosen authentication method

From: DfE Sign-in Team

Published: 3rd October 2023

Last updated: 4th October 2023

Journey overview

A key part of MFA is allowing users to recover their accoun should they be locked out due to the user not being able to access their MFA methods.

This journey shows the recovery journey for how users can recover their MFA via a reset or disable request in order to get access to their DfE Sign-in account.

All users are required to have the ability to recover their account however dpending on their permission level they will need to request it from either an approver or the DfE support team with users with higher permission levels required to request a reset from the support team.

Who can access this journey?

  • DfE Sign-in end users who access at least 1 service with MFA switched on
  • All approver users in DfE Sign-in
  • All Manage Console users
  • All Support Console users
