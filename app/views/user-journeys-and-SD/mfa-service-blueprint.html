Multi-factor authentication overview

Multi-factor authentication is a security feature which is required to be implemented for users on DfE sign-in to protect DfE sign-in as a service and to protect the data and information held by services which use DfE sign-in.

Users a re required to enter a code recieved to another registered device to confirm their identity and login. Users can dit and change their authentication methods and can choose from 3 different authentication methods to register to their account.

Who can access MFA?

  • End users of DfE Sign-in
  • Approvers of DfE Sign-in
  • Manage Console users
  • Support Console users of DfE Sign-in
