Manage users

You can use the user management tool to search, manage and support your service’s existing users and invite new users to your service.

Search for users

On DfE manage console, you can search for all DfE Sign-in users, including those who are existing users of your service.

To search for all DfE Sign-in users:

  1. Sign into your DfE Sign-in account.
  2. Once signed in, select ‘DfE Sign-in manage’ from your list of services.
  3. Choose which service you would like to manage.
  4. Select ‘Users’ from the top navigation bar or click ‘Manage users’ on the DfE manage console dashboard.
  5. Enter a user’s name, email address, legacy ID or organisation into the search box. Click ‘search’.

Search all users

If you want to search for all DfE Sign-in users, check that the text under the search box says ‘Currently viewing all users with access to DfE Sign-in’. If it says ‘Currently viewing all users of [service name]’, click the ‘Show all users’ link.

Search for users of your service

If you want to search for users of your service, check that the text under the search box says ‘Currently viewing all users of [service name]’. If it says ‘Currently viewing all users with access to DfE Sign-in’, click the ‘Only show users with access to my service’ link.

Search for users of your service

You can also refine your search with the filtering tool by clicking ‘Show filter’. You can filter users by:

  • user type
  • organisation type
  • last login
  • account status

When you make a filter selection and move to another page, the selections you made will remain the same.

If you select ‘Clear filters’, the filtering tool will remove all your selections.

View user details

Select an individual user from the list of search results to view their details.

On a user’s profile you will find their:

  • account status
  • email address
  • last login
  • organisation details (including legacy ID, legacy username and permission level)
  • service access and roles
  • approvers

You can also view a user’s sign in audit by following the link under ‘Related actions’.

Invite new users

On a user’s profile, you can also give them access to a service.

To invite new users to your service:

  1. Sign into your DfE Sign-in account.
  2. Once signed in, select ‘DfE Sign-in manage’ from your list of services.
  3. Choose which service you would like to manage.
  4. Select ‘Users’ from the top navigation bar or click ‘Manage users’ on the DfE manage console dashboard.
  5. Search and select the user you want to invite.
  6. On the user’s profile, click ‘Give access to service’.
  7. Select the sub-services or roles that you want to add to the user’s account.
  8. Review the details and click ‘Add service’.

Approvers at the organisation will be notified that you added a service to the user’s account.
