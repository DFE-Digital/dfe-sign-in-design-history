About DfE manage console

DfE manage console is an application for service teams to manage their services.

On DfE manage console, you can:

  • view service information
  • configure a service
  • create service banners
  • manage roles and policies
  • manage user accounts

Access the application

To access DfE manage console:

  1. Sign into your DfE Sign-in account.
  2. Once signed in, select ‘DfE Sign-in manage’ from your list of services.
  3. Choose which service you would like to manage.
  4. You will be taken to your selected service’s DfE manage console dashboard.

On the dashboard, you will see the tiles that allow you to support your service’s users and view and configure your service.

To see another service’s DfE manage console dashboard, click ‘Change service’ under ‘Related actions’.

Contact us

You can contact DfE Sign-in for further support, including how to:

  • change a service title
  • change a service description
  • add someone to DfE manage console
  • add or modify a group or role in DfE manage console
